Overview

Clubs (internally called Pulse) was an event sharing platform within Probo that created communities around prediction events. Users could join public clubs (admin-curated content with captions) or private clubs (any member can share events).

The Vision: Turn event discovery into a social experience. Instead of browsing a catalog, users get curated events from communities they trust.

My Role

  • Designed the club creation and membership flows
  • Built the public vs private club permission system
  • Created the event sharing experience with admin captions
  • Developed the Pulse discovery page
  • Tracked engagement metrics and iterated on UX

Key Features

  • Public Clubs: Admin-curated events with editorial captions ‚Äî quality over quantity
  • Private Clubs: Any member can share events ‚Äî community-driven content discovery
  • Pulse Page: Central discovery hub for all club activity
  • Social Sharing: Easy share flows to bring friends into clubs
