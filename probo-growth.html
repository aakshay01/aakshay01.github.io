Overview

SKU Optimization was about strategically expanding event coverage while consolidating liquidity where it mattered. For Cricket, we pushed multiple league questions and optimized based on traction. For categories like Football and Basketball, we consolidated liquidity into "hero questions" to ensure deep markets.

The Strategy: Expand breadth in high-demand categories (Cricket), consolidate depth in emerging categories (Football, Basketball) ‚Äî maximize liquidity utilization across the platform.

My Role

  • Analyzed traction data across leagues and question types
  • Defined SKU expansion strategy for Cricket (IPL, international, domestic leagues)
  • Identified hero questions for liquidity consolidation
  • Built feedback loops to optimize based on trading volume
  • Coordinated with ops on event creation priorities

Approach

  • Cricket Expansion: Multiple leagues, multiple question types ‚Äî chase the traction, scale what works
  • Hero Questions: For Football/Basketball, identify the single best question per match and consolidate all liquidity there
  • Data-Driven: Continuous optimization based on volume, spread, and user engagement metrics
  • Seasonal Adjustments: Ramp up/down based on league calendars
